About me

I'm a Marshallese Software Engineer based in North Carolina

I started my career in software development because of an innate passion to break, build and explore. Years of being curious about the inner workings of video games sparked a flame that has pushed me to figure out the how and why behind computer science, and now I have harnessed that passion to build out the projects that I have dreamt about building since I was a child.

Being able to provide a fulfilling product that I can infuse my creativity, critical thinking and passions into simple, elegant and innovative solutions, is what I love to do.

When I'm not developing websites, you can find me doing various coding challenges, developing & play-testing video games, enjoying other hobbies of mine or just hanging around with my family.
